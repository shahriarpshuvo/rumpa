## Hook (2-3 sentences)
- Open with the patient question many women bring to me after a PCOS diagnosis: "Does PCOS make IVF more or less likely to work?" Reassure that IVF with PCOS success rate data is broadly encouraging, women with PCOS typically reach similar or better live birth rates per cycle, but the path needs a tailored protocol because of OHSS risk and egg quality nuance.

## What the Data Actually Shows ← targets: "IVF with PCOS success rate"
- Per-cycle live birth rates similar to age-matched non-PCOS peers
- Cumulative live birth often higher because of more eggs collected (more embryos to bank)
- A large Chinese cohort suggests cumulative live birth peaks around 15-25 retrieved oocytes in PCOS, plateaus beyond
- Mature-egg ratio (MII) tends to be lower in PCOS, so total yield ≠ usable embryos
- `<Cite n="1" />` Reprod Biol Endocrinol 2023 review

## The "High Yield" Phenomenon, Why It Cuts Both Ways ← targets: "egg yield PCOS IVF"
- Antral follicle count is high, ovaries recruit many follicles
- Risk: oversuppression on standard doses → OHSS, immature eggs
- Lower starting FSH doses are routine in PCOS protocols
- `<Callout type="clinical-tip">` More eggs is good news; OHSS-free more eggs is the goal

## OHSS Risk and How Modern Protocols Manage It ← targets: "OHSS risk PCOS IVF"
- Severe OHSS rate ~15% in PCOS vs ~3% in non-PCOS without prevention
- GnRH antagonist cycles + GnRH agonist trigger (instead of hCG) substantially reduce OHSS
- Pre-treatment with metformin reduces OHSS risk
- Vitamin D repletion supports response
- Red flags after retrieval: rapid weight gain, breathlessness, severe bloating, reduced urine output
- `<Cite n="2" />` Minimising OHSS in PCOS, PMC 2024

## Freeze-All Strategy for PCOS ← targets: "freeze-all strategy PCOS"
- Why: trigger + retrieval leaves ovaries hyperresponsive; fresh transfer adds pregnancy hormones → worse OHSS
- Freeze all embryos, transfer in a later, controlled cycle
- Implantation rates often higher in FET for PCOS because uterine environment is more receptive
- `<Cite n="3" />` ESHRE / PCOS IVF review

## Which Protocol Is Best for IVF with PCOS ← targets: "what protocol is best for IVF with PCOS"
- Antagonist protocol with agonist trigger and freeze-all is the modern default
- Metformin co-treatment in insulin-resistant patients
- Patient question to ask: "What is my predicted OHSS risk and how are you mitigating it?"
- Does PCOS make IVF more or less likely to work, plain answer: similar per-cycle, often better cumulative, with the right protocol
