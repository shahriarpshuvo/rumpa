# Product Profile

## Overview
Dr. Rezwana Rumpa is a practising OB-GYN whose practice and content are built around **TTC (Trying To Conceive)**. She supports women and couples through the full conception journey — from pre-conception optimisation, to diagnosing why conception isn't happening, to managing fertility-adjacent conditions (PCOS/PMOS), navigating IVF decisions, and protecting the resulting pregnancy (gestational diabetes care). Online telemedicine consultations + a doctor-authored content platform fill the trust gap left by app-based fertility content and patient-forum advice.

## Pain Points
- **Months of failed TTC with no clear answer:** Couples have been trying for 6–24+ months, cycled through ovulation apps and OTC advice, and don't know what to investigate next, what tests to ask for, or when to escalate to IVF
- **PCOS/PMOS as a TTC blocker:** Diagnosed (or suspected) PCOS patients confused about which interventions actually improve conception odds — Metformin vs Inositol vs Letrozole vs lifestyle — and what "lean PCOS" means for their TTC timeline
- **Protecting a hard-won pregnancy:** Once conception happens after a long TTC journey, gestational diabetes (and other pregnancy complications) feel even higher-stakes; patients want a doctor who already knows their fertility history

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Primary segment:** Women 28–40 actively trying to conceive (with or without a diagnosis); couples planning a family who want to optimise before TTC
- **Secondary segments:** Women with PCOS/PMOS worried about fertility timing; TTC patients evaluating IVF; women who finally conceived (often after a long TTC journey) now managing gestational diabetes
- **Geographic skew:** UK + US + South Asian diaspora in English-speaking countries
- **Technical Sophistication:** Non-technical / semi-technical — health-literate, research-savvy patients who Google their symptoms and compare doctors
- **Stage:** Mid-funnel — already aware of their TTC challenge, searching for clarity, second opinions, and actionable next steps

## USP (Unique Selling Proposition)
A practising OB-GYN whose entire practice is organised around the TTC journey — pre-conception, conception, and protecting the resulting pregnancy. PCOS/PMOS is treated as a fertility pathway (not a standalone endocrine issue), IVF is positioned as one option among many (not the default), and gestational diabetes is framed as protecting a hard-won pregnancy. Evidence-based clinical authority that app-driven content (Flo, Premom) and patient forums cannot match.

## Audience & Geo
- **Primary Markets:** UK, US, secondary global English-speaking (Canada, Australia, Ireland, English-speaking South Asian diaspora)
- **Languages:** English only (v1)
- **Regional Considerations:**
  - UK readers expect NHS-aware context (referral pathways, NICE guidance)
  - US readers expect insurance + IVF cost framing
  - South Asian diaspora readers — culturally sensitive content around family pressure, gender preference, late-marriage TTC
