## Hook (2-3 sentences)
- If your GP or fertility clinic has offered you tablets to bring on ovulation, the letrozole vs clomid PCOS question is usually the first thing patients ask me about. Both drugs work by nudging your brain to push out more FSH, but the live-birth data and the side effect profile are not the same. Here is how I talk patients through the choice.

## What letrozole and clomid actually do ← targets: "letrozole ovulation induction"
- Quick mechanism in plain English: aromatase inhibitor (letrozole) vs selective estrogen receptor modulator (clomiphene citrate PCOS).
- Half-life difference, why letrozole leaves the endometrium and cervical mucus alone.
- `<Callout type="clinical-tip">` "Neither drug is a fertility booster for a healthy cycle, they are for women who are not ovulating reliably."

## Why letrozole became the first-line PCOS ovulation drug ← targets: "first-line PCOS ovulation drug"
- The 2014 NEJM PPCOS II trial, live-birth rate 27.5% letrozole vs 19.1% clomiphene `<Cite n="1" />`.
- 2018 international PCOS guideline (endorsed by ESHRE, ASRM, Monash) moved letrozole to first-line `<Cite n="2" />`.
- 2024 PMC meta-analysis confirming higher ovulation, pregnancy and live-birth with letrozole `<Cite n="3" />`.

## Side effects, twins risk and what to expect each cycle ← targets: "what are the side effects of clomid vs letrozole"
- Clomid: hot flushes, mood changes, thinner endometrium, drier cervical mucus, higher twin rate (~7-10%).
- Letrozole: tiredness, mild headaches, lower multiple rate (~3-4%), no antiestrogenic uterine effects.
- Note on the old fetal-malformation scare and why it was disproven `<Cite n="4" />`.
- `<Callout type="note">` Monitoring expectations: day-3 baseline, mid-cycle scan, day-21 progesterone.

## Is letrozole better than clomid for PCOS in every case? ← targets: "is letrozole better than clomid for PCOS"
- Honest nuance: clomid still has a role if letrozole has failed, in some funding pathways, or when a clinician prefers the longer evidence base.
- BMI, age, insulin resistance and AMH all change the conversation.
- Combined letrozole + clomid protocols, what the 2025 meta-analysis shows `<Cite n="5" />`.

## UK and US prescribing: who can give you what ← bridges to related Rumpa post
- UK: letrozole is off-label for ovulation induction, usually prescribed by a fertility specialist after NHS referral or privately. NICE NG257 sets the framework `<Cite n="6" />`.
- US: letrozole is widely used off-label, often started by REI or general OB-GYN.
- Link to [PCOS and TTC, Tips That Help](/blog/pcos-ttc-tips) and [NHS Fertility Testing, What You Get and When](/blog/nhs-fertility-testing) for the referral route.

## What This Means for You
- The letrozole vs clomid PCOS choice is rarely 50/50 in 2026, letrozole now wins on live births and on side effects for most women with PCOS. That said, the right drug depends on your phenotype, your previous cycles and where you sit in the NHS or US pathway.
