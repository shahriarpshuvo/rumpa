## Hook (2-3 sentences)
- Open with the reader's question: many patients ask what to do 3 months before trying to conceive, and the honest answer is that the egg released the month you conceive started maturing 90 days earlier. The choices you make now sit inside that egg. This post walks through the 12-week window step by step.

## Pre-conception supplements, what to start now ← targets: "pre-conception supplements"
- Folic acid: NHS 400 mcg vs higher 5 mg dose if BMI greater than 30, diabetes, epilepsy meds, neural tube history
- Vitamin D 10 mcg (UK standard), iodine, iron only if low
- CoQ10 and omega-3 evidence quality
- "Does diet 3 months before pregnancy matter?" answer in prose with Mediterranean pattern data
- `<Callout type="note">` Buy a prenatal that meets your country's guideline, not the highest-priced one

## What to stop: alcohol, caffeine, smoking, and certain meds ← targets: "stop alcohol caffeine pre-TTC"
- Alcohol: NHS guidance is zero in pregnancy, plan to stop now
- Caffeine: keep under 200 mg/day pre-TTC
- Smoking and vaping: cessation halves miscarriage risk, free NHS Stop Smoking support
- Medication audit with your GP (acne meds, mental health meds, herbal supplements)

## Tests and check-ups to schedule in months 1-2 ← targets: "what to start 3 months before TTC"
- Cervical screening if due
- Rubella and varicella immunity check
- Thyroid (TSH), HbA1c if PCOS or family history of diabetes
- Dental check, BP, BMI conversation
- For UK readers: GP pre-conception appointment, for US readers: well-woman visit and insurance check

## Lifestyle resets: sleep, stress, movement, weight ← targets: "what to do 3 months before trying to conceive"
- 7-9 hours sleep, melatonin's link to ovarian antioxidant pathways
- Stress: not "relax and it will happen", but cortisol's measurable effect on the HPG axis
- 150 minutes moderate movement per week
- Weight conversation handled without shame, BMI 19-29 is the lowest-risk band for conception
- Bridge to related Rumpa post on weight and pre-conception fertility

## What This Means for You
- Three months before trying to conceive is the most useful window you have. Take folic acid, line up tests, cut alcohol and tobacco, and protect sleep.
